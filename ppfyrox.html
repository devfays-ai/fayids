Privacy Policy

Last Modified: April 26, 2026

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of data Fyrox ("the Bot") collects, how it is used, and the steps we take to protect your information.

1. Data We Collect

To provide its services, Fyrox collects minimal data necessary for functionality. This includes:

  • User Identifiers: Your Discord User ID is stored to keep track of your economy balance, levels, and bot preferences.
  • Server Identifiers: Guild IDs and Channel IDs are stored to save server-specific configurations (e.g., custom prefixes, log channels, welcome messages).
  • Message Content: The bot processes message content to detect commands and run moderation filters. We do not log or store your chat history permanently in our databases.
  • Usage Logs: Commands used and timestamps may be logged temporarily to help us debug issues and prevent spam.

2. How We Use Your Data

The information we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the features of the Bot (e.g., loading your RPG profile).
  • To enforce server safety through moderation tools (e.g., tracking warns/mutes).
  • To analyze bot performance and improve user experience.

3. Data Retention & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. Data is stored in secure databases (such as MongoDB) with restricted access. We retain your data as long as you are a user of the bot. If the bot is removed from a server, server-specific configuration may be kept for a grace period before being purged.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. Data is only shared when required by law or to comply with Discord's official requests.

5. User Rights (Data Deletion)

You have the right to request the deletion of your data. If you wish to have your profile and all associated data wiped from our records, you may:

  • Use the /delete-my-data command (if available).
  • Join our Support Server and open a ticket with the developers.
  • Contact us via email at [your-email@example.com].

6. External Links

If the bot provides links to external websites (e.g., dashboard, documentation), we are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage users to check this page periodically. Continued use of the bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
